Golden Triangle Tour

The Golden Triangle is India’s leading tourism circuit — for a reason. This “triangle” of destinations includes the historic city of Delhi, which boasts three UNESCO World Heritage sites; Agra, home of the Taj Mahal, and Jaipur, the Pink City.

Shopping in India Tour

India is a shopper’s paradise. There is an astonishing variety of dazzling clothes, fabrics, shawls, shoes, jewellery, spices, rugs, pottery, furniture, and much, much more to buy in India. The tour is designed to be a fun and interactive cultural experience.
